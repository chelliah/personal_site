Fuschia

A site for buying and selling used items of clothing

Problem:

I love used clothing. As someone who changes their wardrobe frequently, buying, selling, and trading items of clothing is an important part of how I think about fashion. Consignment and used clothing stores are great places to find unique items, but buying clothing from friends or neighbors is often a hassle.

Solution:

I developed a web application that makes buying and selling used items of clothing a little bit easier. Users can upload an image and description of the item of clothing they wish to sell. Users looking to buy an item can search by size, type, gender, or condition, and send an email to the seller via a built in email service.
