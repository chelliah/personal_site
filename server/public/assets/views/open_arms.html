Open Arms Meal Picker

A meal selection appilcation developed for Open Arms of Minnesota

Problem:

Open Arms of Minnesota cooks and delivers free, nutritious meals to people living with life-threatening and chronic illness in the Twin Cities. With the help of more than 2,400 volunteers, they deliver over half a million meals a year, serving upwards of 1000 clients weekly. Under their current model, clients are unable to choose the meals they have delivered to their homes each week. Open Arms wanted to provide an option to give clients more freedom to eat the meals they wanted.

Solution:

A web application to allow clients to customize the meals they have delivered to their home, while providing tools to ease the administrative burden at open arms. Our site contains an administrative page and a client page, both accessible from the main login. On the client page, a client can select up to five meals to have delivered for the given week. We wanted to make sure this change would be easily managed by administrators at Open Arms, so we provided an administrative page that manages clients, meals, and menus. On this page, administrators can add and remove clients, create and view meals, create and edit menus, and get information about client orders for each week.
